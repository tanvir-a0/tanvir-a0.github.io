### Recommended Format:
- **File name**: `profile.jpg` or `profile.webp`
- **Dimensions**: 400x400px to 600x600px (square is best)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (square) or 3:4 (portrait)
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for fast loading
- **Format**: JPEG (.jpg) or WebP (.webp)

### Image Quality:
- **Resolution**: Use 2x the display size for retina screens (e.g., 360x360 displays as 180x180)
- **DPI**: 144 DPI or higher
- **Compression**: 85-90% quality for JPEG

### Photography Tips:
1. **Professional Look**: Clean background, good lighting
2. **Composition**: Center yourself, leave some headroom
3. **Lighting**: Natural light or soft studio lighting
4. **Expression**: Friendly, approachable smile
5. **Attire**: Professional or smart casual
6. **Background**: Solid color or subtle gradient (white, gray, or blurred)

### How to Add:
1. Place your image in the `/assets/` folder
2. Name it `profile.jpg` (or update the HTML if using a different name)
3. The image will automatically appear in the hero section with effects
