About Me

I am very interested in 3D printing and have used a lot of 3D-printed parts in various projects. Apart from using the 3D printers at my home for projects, I try to print cool stuff and contribute to the global community. I also have experience fixing printers. For example, the CR-6 SE and FlashForge Creator Pro were badly damaged, so I fixed them and then used them.
